**Description:** Configure the PCRE regular expression recursion depth limit.

**Syntax:** `SecPcreMatchLimitRecursion LIMIT`

**Default:** 1000

**Case Sensitive:** Yes

**Implemented:** No

**Example:**

```apache
SecPcreMatchLimitRecursion 1000
```
